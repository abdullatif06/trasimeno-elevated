
# Trasimeno Coffee — Premium Website Redesign

A clean, modern, and polished single-page website for Trasimeno Coffee with rich animations and interactive elements.

---

## 1. Navigation Bar
- Sticky, transparent-on-scroll navigation that becomes solid on scroll
- Trasimeno Coffee logo/brand name with elegant serif typography
- Smooth scroll links: Home, Menu, Gallery, Location, Contact
- Mobile hamburger menu with slide-in drawer

## 2. Hero Section
- Full-screen hero with a large coffee house background image
- Parallax scrolling effect on the background
- Animated headline: "Great Food & Strong Coffee" with staggered letter/word reveal
- Subtitle and a CTA button with hover animation
- Subtle scroll-down indicator

## 3. About Us Section
- Split layout: text on one side, image on the other
- Three feature cards (Quality, Atmosphere, More than Drinks) with icons
- Cards animate in on scroll with staggered fade-up effect
- Clean typography with generous spacing

## 4. Menu Showcase
- Tabbed categories (Hot Drinks, Iced Drinks, Food, Desserts)
- Each item displayed as a card with name, description, and price
- Smooth tab transition animations
- Hover effects on menu cards (subtle scale + shadow)
- Link to full digital menu for complete offerings

## 5. Gallery Section
- Masonry or grid photo gallery showcasing the coffee house atmosphere
- Images with hover zoom/overlay effect
- Lightbox modal when clicking an image
- Scroll-triggered fade-in animations for each image

## 6. Location & Hours
- Store location with embedded map placeholder
- Opening hours displayed in a clean card layout
- Contact information (phone, email)
- Animated on-scroll entrance

## 7. Footer
- Brand name and tagline
- Quick navigation links
- Social media icons with hover effects
- Copyright notice
- Links to terms and conditions

## 8. Design & Polish
- **Color palette**: Clean whites and light grays with dark accents, subtle coffee-toned highlights
- **Typography**: Elegant serif for headings, clean sans-serif for body
- **Animations**: Scroll-triggered fade-ins, parallax hero, hover scale effects, smooth page transitions
- **Responsive**: Fully mobile-optimized with adapted layouts
- **Performance**: Smooth 60fps animations throughout
